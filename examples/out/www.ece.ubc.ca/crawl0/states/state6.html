    <h3>Welcome to UBC's Electrical and Computer Engineering Program</h3><p>Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) offers exciting opportunities for students and faculty in the rapidly evolving fields of information and communication technology and the latest advances in the more traditional areas of power, control, and electromagnetics.</p><p>Undergraduate students can choose from several program options including project-based and cooperative education programs. Our graduate school offers two Master's programs and a Doctoral program and currently enrolls over 350 students from many countries.</p>
